Studio Reference Series

Crystal Clear Audio Cables presents the Studio Reference Series, a line of product of Interconnects and Power cables in improved configurations and new design using 99.999% Silver (5n's) conductor and the best connectors in the industry.

Beautiful cosmetic design, well quality built, A true Audiophile piece and the sound is incredibly amazing. Combining the best Audiophile cables with the best connectors in the market. Japanese made Furutech connectors and finest Oyaide power plugs. Along with US made Cardas Audio. Only the best parts and material are selected.

Guaranteed significant sonic improvement. Transparency and musical detail with the most extreme accuracy, raised the standard of interconnect, speaker cable and power cables design to a new heights.
